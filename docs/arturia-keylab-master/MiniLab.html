ARTURIA – MINILAB

Important note: For the script to work, you need to load the "Default" Template into the controller with the Arturia MIDI Control Center.
GLOBAL
PAD 1-8
(LED Off)		 Normal Mode.
Pads are sending notes.
PAD 9-16
(LED On)		 Controller Mode.
Pads are used for controlling Bitwig Studio.
PAD 1: MIX MODE
PAD 5 Submode
Volume and Pan:
E 1-8 Track volume.
E 9-16 Track pan.
PAD 6 Submode
Sends:
E 1-8 Track Send 1.
E 9-16 Track Send 2.
PAD 7 & 8 Move track window.
PAD 2 / DEVICE MODE
E 1-8 Device macros.
E 9-16 Device mapping: Common parameters.
PAD 5 & 6 Select cursor track.
PAD 7 & 8 Select cursor device.
PAD 3 / PRESET MODE
E 1-8 Device mapping: Envelope parameters.
E 9-16 Device mapping: User parameters.
PAD 5 & 6 Select user parameter page.
PAD 7 & 8 Select device preset.
PAD 4 / ARTURIA/USER MODE
E 1-8 Arturia Analog Lab/User mappings.
E 9-16 Arturia Analog Lab/User mappings.
PAD 5 - 8 Arturia Analog Lab snapshots.
